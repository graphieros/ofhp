Alec Lloyd Probert

Front-end open-source developer

Alec Lloyd Probert is a front-end software engineer based in France, working mainly on data visualization and interactive web interfaces with JavaScript and Vue.js. Alongside his development work, he explores visual art and language creation through graphieros, a project combining hexagonal geometry, painting, and a constructed language. He also maintains open-source projects such as vue-data-ui, focused on turning complex data into clear, usable interfaces. His work sits at the intersection of software development and creative practice.
